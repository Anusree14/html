Samsung Galaxy S22

The Samsung Galaxy S-series comprises a series of high-end Andriod mobile devices by the South Korean giant.The latest addition to the ever-increasing line-up of S-series smartphones is the Galaxy S22.The phone flaunts a 6.2-inches Dynamic AMOLED with a resolution of 1440 x 3200 pixels and a high pixel density of 566ppi.

The phone gets a bezel-less punch-hole framework at the front, with a Corning Gorilla Glass protection.

Also, the phone gets an IP68 rating, making it waterproof and dustproof. On the optical front, Samsung is offering a triple camera setup (64MP+12MP+64MP) on the rear with an LED flash. For selfies, the smartphone includes a 16MP camera at the front. The listed storage capacity of the phone is 128GB, and it is non-expandable. In terms of performance, the handset is powered by the Samsung Exynos 2100 octa-core processor clubbed with 8GB RAM.
